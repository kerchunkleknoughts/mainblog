main ship



This is the main ship, before I beat the mechhive it was designed to be fully equipped as a functional base, but post mechhive I moved all of my stuff to a space base on the hive itself. Photos of that will be below. Currently this ship serves to collect wood and steel from tiles on the planet. Hence the massive amount of storage containers you see in the image. I keep a few pawns on the ship during refuel missions and have my mechs go out and collect stuff. This ensures that no pawns get injured during refuel missions. Also, I have a massive array of autocannons at the front of the ship. I got the idea from this post

main ship blueprint

here is the layout of the ship. Everything is set up so that the required inputs for each room are in one of its neighboring rooms.

moon base

Here is the moon base! I have pawns who are best at crafting live here while the main ship goes out to collect resources. This way, I can free up space on the main ship that used to be detecated towards production. you may need to zoom in on your browser to see all of the detail of this image.
