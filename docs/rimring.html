What is rim ring?

Rim ring is a webring based on the hit 2013 video game rim world! Have you lost countless weeks upon weeks playing this game? Has time become a meaningless construct as your thoughts become only of your colony? If so, this webring is for you!

How do I join?

To join rimring, fill out the form located here, members are encouraged to also have a link to a photo of their favorite rimworld base, or of a post talking about their rimworld base.
